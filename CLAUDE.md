# Workshop: AI in the Newsroom

## Purpose

Practical workshop on using AI tools in journalism — focused on **quality, security, and efficiency** in AI-powered newsroom workflows. Helps journalists adopt AI responsibly.

## Target Audience

- **Level**: Working journalists and editors
- **Prior knowledge**: Basic familiarity with AI chatbots (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- **Goal**: Integrate AI tools into daily journalistic practice without compromising quality or ethics

## Structure

- **10 lessons** covering AI tools, prompting, verification, ethics
- **Interface languages**: Deutsch, English, Arabic, Farsi
- **Teaching language**: matches interface language
- **Features**: practical examples, case studies, ethical considerations

## Labels

`IT`

## Conventions

- Real-world journalism scenarios
- Emphasis on verification and fact-checking
- Ethical guidelines for AI-assisted reporting
- `q` = scenario/question, `a` = best practice answer
